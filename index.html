Brazilian Flan

Discover the Sweetness of Brazilian Flan

Pudim Brasileiro, also known as Brazilian flan, is a delightful caramel dessert enjoyed by people all around the world. Its smooth texture and rich flavor make it a favorite treat for all ages.


Nutritional Information

One serving of Brazilian Flan (approximately 150g) contains:

  • Calories: 250 kcal
  • Total Fat: 12g
  • Saturated Fat: 7g
  • Cholesterol: 100mg
  • Sodium: 80mg
  • Total Carbohydrates: 32g
  • Dietary Fiber: 0g
  • Sugars: 30g
  • Protein: 6g
